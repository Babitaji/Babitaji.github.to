ISI WAJAH SE BABITA USSE NARAZ REHTI H KYUN KI HAMARI SEX BOMB KO TO CHUDAI HI CHUDAI KARNI H..

USKI CHUT KO TO SIRF LUND CHAHIYE HOTA H..IYER PURI TARAH THAK CHUKA THA..AUR WO SOFE PAR BAITH GAYA AUR APNA LUND KO KHADE KARNE KI KOSIS KAR RAHA THA..

AIYEE ANDAR CHALTE H..

JHETHALAL AB HOTH CHUSNA BAND KARTA H AUR ANJALI K BOOBS CHUSNA CHALU KARTA H...KABHI RIGHT WALA CHUSTA H TO KABHI LEFT WALA..

BABITA : 1 MIN DAYA BHABHI.

MADHVI BHABHI UNGLI MAT DALIYE ANDAR CUPBOARD MEIN PLASTIC KA LUND PADA H WO DALIYE..

MADHVI : THIK H AUR WO KHADI HOTI H AUR CUPBOARD MEIN SE PLASTIC KA KALA LUND NIKALTI H KARIB 14 YA 15 INCH KA LAG RAHA THA..

BABITA NE TO DAYA K HOTH CHUSNA CHALU KAR DIYA THA..MADHVI FIR LUND KO PAKADTI H AUR BABITA KI CHUT PAR RAKHTI H AUR RAGADNE LAGTI H..

AUR THODI DER BAAD EK HI JHATKE MEIN PURA ANDAR GHUSA DETI H..

BABITA KO BAHOT DARD HUA PAR WO CHILA TO NAHI SAKI PAR USNE JOR SE DAYA K HOTH PAR KAT LIYA..

DAYA KO KHOON NIKAL NE LAGA..JAISE HI BABITA NE USKE HOTH CHUSNA BAND KIYA..

DAYA JOR SE CHILAYI..JHETHALAL AND ANJALI NE TO JYADA DYAN NAHI DIYA WO TO MAZE KAR RAHE THE..

PAR HAMARE KALI SCIENTIST SAHAB BHAGTE HUE DARWAJE PAR AKAR JHANKNE LAGE..

AUR JAISE HI ANDAR DEKHA USKE TO HOSH UDD GAYE AUR SOCHNE LAGA SALI RANDI ITNA KALA AUR BADA LUND LE RAHI H..MANA K MERA BHI KALA H PAR USKE JITNA NAHI H ISLIYE WO MERA NAHI LE RAHI H..

KHER ISE CHODTE H ANDAR JAKAR DEKHTE H K HAMARI DAYA KO KUCH HUA TO NAHI NA..DAYA KO KHUN NIKAL RAHA THA SIDHA KHUN TAPKA BABITA K HOTH PAR.BABITA USE PI GAYI AND JAISE HI DAYA KUCH BOLNE WALI HOTI H K BABITA USE PAKAD TI H AUR USKE HOTH CHUSNE LAGTI H..

AUR USKA SARA KHUN PINE LAGTI H..

DAYA KO THODI THANDAK HOTI H..

AUR WO BHI USKA SATH DENE LAGTI H..

MADHVI AB STROKE LAGANA CHALU KAR DETI H..

AIYE CHALTE H JHETHALAL K PASS..JHETHALAL NE ANJALI KO 69 POSTION ME LE LIYA THA..AUR JHETHALAL ANJALI KI CHUT CHATNE LAGA ANJALI JHETHALAL KA LUND MUH ME LEKAR ANDAR BAHAR KARNE LAGI..

KARIB 20 MIN TAK KARNE K BAAD ANJALI JHETHALAL K MUH PAR JHAD JATI H..JHETHALAL USKA SARA RAS PI JATA H..

PAR HAMARA JHETHALAL JHADA NAHI THA..ANJALI USKA LUND AB FAST CHUSNE LAGI...KARIB 7 MIN TAK CHUSNE K BAAD JHETHALAL ANJALI K MUH K ANDAR JHAD JATA H.ANJALI USKA SARA RAS PI JATI H..AUR JHETHALAL K LUND KO KHADA KARNE KI KOSIS KARTI H.

AUR USKE LUND KI SAWARI KARNE LAGTI H..
