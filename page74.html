AB AAGE

BAPUJI : BAHU MERI SHART YE H KI MUJHE TUMHARE SATH SEX KARNA H..

YE SUNKAR TARAK AUR DAYA DONO K MUH SE KYAAA NIKALTA H.

DAYA : BAPUJI YE AAP KYA BOL RAHE?.

BAPUJI : HA BETA ME SACH BOL RAHA HUN MENE KAI BAAR TUMHARE NAAM KI MUTH MARI H.

DAYA : BAPUJI ME AAP K SATH KAISE KAR SAKTI HUN AAP MERE BAPUJI HO.

BAPUJI : TO KYA HUA BAHU BAPUJI SAMAJKAR NAHI PAR EK MARD AUR EK AURAT SAMAJKAR KARLO.DAYA : PAR.

BAPUJI BICH ME HI : PAR WAR KUCH NAHI BAHU MUJHE TUMHARE SATH SEX KARNA HI H.

DAYA : BAPUJI MUJHE 2 MIN DIJIYE ME SOCH KAR BATATI HUN.

BAPUJI : THIK H.

DAYA : MAHETASAHEB KITCHEN ME CHALIYE.

TARAK : THIK H.

BAPUJI : BAHU WAHA MAHETA SE CHIPAK MAT JANA JALDI AANA.

DAYA : THIK H.AUR TARAK DAYA ANDAR JATE H.

DAYA : MAHETASAHEB YE BAPUJI KYA KEH RAHE H.

TARAK : HA DAYA BHABHI ME BHI HAIRAN REH GAYA.

DAYA : TO ME AB KYA KARU?.

TARAK : DAYA BHABHI AAP BAPUJI K SATH KAR LIJIYE.

DAYA : HEY MA MATAJI YE AAP KYA BOL RAHE H.

TARAK : ME SACH BOL RAHA HUN KYUN KI AAP KARLOGI BAPUJI K SATH TO HUM FIR KHULE AAM KAR SAKENGE

AUR JHETHALAL,TAPPU,ANJALI AND BABITA JI BHI KAR SAKENGE.

DAYA : THIK H MAHETASAHEB ME KARUNGI.

TARAK : HA TO CHALIYE BAHAR AUR SHURU HO JAIYE.DAYA TARAK DONO BAHAR JATE H.

DAYA : BAPUJI ME TAIYAR HUN.

AUR YE SUNTE HI BAPUJI AGE BADTE H AUR DAYA KO PAKADTE H AUR APNE HOTH DAYA K GULABI HOTHON PAR RAKHKAR CHUSNE LAGTE H.

DAYA BHI BAPUJI KA SATH DE RAHI THI.

BAPUJI TO DAYA K AISE HOTH CHUS RAHE THE KI KISI BACCHE KO BAHOT DIN BAAD LOLLYPOP MILI HO.

TARAK YE DONO KO DEKHKAR UTTEJIT HO GAYA THA ISLIYE WO KHURSI PAR BAITHKAR APNA LUND NIKALKAR HILA RAHA THA.FIR BAPUJI HOTH CHUSNA BAND KARTE H AUR DAYA KI SARI NIKALNE LAGTE H.

DAYA SHARMA RAHI THI KYUN KI USKI SARI USKE SASURJI NIKAL RAHE THE.

AB BAPUJI NE DAYA KI SARI NIKAL KAR PHENK DI.DAYA SIRF PETICOAT AUR BLOUSE ME HI THI.

KYA LAG RAHI THI DAYA.EK DUM HOT AND SEXY.AB BAPUJI DAYA K DO BADE BADE 34 SIZE K BOOBS PAKAD KAR JOR JOR SE DABANE LAGTE H.

TARAK KI HALAT KHARAB HO GAYI THI WO TO 2 BAAR JHAD CHUKA THA.

DAYA AHAHAHAHAHAHAHUHAHUHAHUHAHUHAHUHAHUAHAUHAHUHAHUGAHUAHUAHUHAUH BAPUJI MAZA AA RAHA H..BAPUJI AB PURE JOSH ME AA GAYE THE..

BAPUJI NE AB APNA KURTA NIKAL DIYA AUR SIRF DHOTI ME AGAYE THE.

FIR UNHONE DAYA KA BLOUSE NIKAL DIYA.DAYA NE BRA NAHI PEHNI THI USKE BOOBS AB AZAD THE.

BAPUJI : BAHU AAJ TO TUJHE ITNE MAZE DUNGA KI TUNE JINDAGI BHAR NAHI LIYE HONGE KITNE DINO K BAAD ME SEX KARUNGA AUR KITNE DINO K BAAD MENE KISI LADKI K BOOBS DEKHE H..

AUR ITNA KEHTE HI BAPUJI APNA MUH DAYA K NIPPLES PAR RAKHKAR CHUSNE LAGTE H.

DAYA AHAHAUHAHUHAUHAUHAUHAUHAUHAHUHAHUHA KAR RAHI THI CHUSIYE BAPUJI MAZA AA RAHA H.

BAPUJI : BAHU TERE DHUDH KA SWADH TO LAJAWAB H AAJ TO TERI PURI DAIRY KHALI KAR DUNGA.

DAYA : NAHI BAPUJI THODA BACCHA KAR RAKHNA DUSRO K LIYE.B

APUJI : KIS K LIYE HAJU KITNO K SATH KARWAYEGI.

DAYA : BAPUJI MERA BAS CHALE TO ME PURE DUNya K MARDO K SATH KARWAUN.

BAPUJI : CHAL RANDI UNSE BAAD ME KARLENA PEHLE MUJHE TO KARNE DE.

DAYA : HA TO KARIYE NA MENE KAB MANA KIYA H.

AUR BAPUJI FIR SE BOOBS CHUSNE LAGTE H KABHI LEFT WALA TO KABHI RIGHT WALA.

FIR BAPUJI NICHE ATE H DAYA KA PETICOAT NIKAL DETE H DAYA KI BAAL WALI CHUT BAPUJI K SAMNE THI.

BAPUJI : BAHU AGAR TU DUSRO SE CHUDWANA CHAHTI H TO PEHLE APNI IS CHUT K BAAL KATLENA.

DAYA : THIK H BAPUJI.PAR AAP MUJHE BHI TO AAPKA LUND CHUSWAIYE.

BAPUJI : HA RUK.AUR BAPUJI APNI DHOTI NIKAL DETE H BAPUJI KA 8 INCH KA LUND SAMNE AA JATA H.YE DEKHKAR

DAYA : WAH BAPUJI AAPKA TO KITNA BADA H AAJ TO MAZA AA JAYEGA..

BAPUJI : HA BAHU LE AB ISE APNE MUH ME.

DAYA : HA BAPUJI AUR DAYA GHUTNO K BAL NICHE BAITH JATI H.AUR BAPUJI KA LUND PAKADKAR HILANE LAGTI H AUR FIR PURA APNE MUH ME DALKAR CHUSNE LAGTI H.JOR JOR SE CHUSNE LAGTI H.

BAPUJI : WAH RANDI KYA LUND CHUS RAHI H MAZA AA RAHA H AUR CHUS JOR SE AHAHAH BAHU WAH.10 MIN CHUSNE K BAAD BAPUJI DAYA K MUH ME HI JHAD JATE H AUR APNA SARA RAS DAYA KO PILA DETE H.FIR

BAPUJI : CHAL BAHU AB PALANG PAR SOH JA AB ME TERI CHUT MARUNGA.

DAYA : THIK H AUR PALANG PAR LAT JATI H BAPUJI APNA LUND KO PAKADTE H AUR USE KHADA KARTE H FIR APNA LUND DAYA KI CHUT PAR RAKHAKAR RAGADNE LAGTE H FIR THODI DER BAAD APNA PURA LUND DAYA KI CHUT ME DAL DETE H.
